Research portfolio.

Academic research with practical relevance.

All my projects have a scholarly and a practical side. At the scholarly level, the idea is to learn about how a variety of organisations from across environment and food sectors tackle their respective policy challenges – to then extract cross-sectoral lessons.

At the practical level, this means ability to help public and private organisations to improve how they tackle pressing policy challenges.
