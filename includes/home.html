Dr Jose A Bolanos.

Mildly extravagant political economist.

Hi! Thanks for visiting. I am a political economist with 5+ years of experience in policy analysis, 2+ years in data-driven roles in the private sector, and extensive engagement with policymakers and stakeholders.

I am passionate about understanding the interconnections between seemingly separate human phenomena. In my work, this translates into research/analysis about how different types of organisations tackle some of the trickiest policy/governance challenges of our days—the final goal being some sort of (fork-join) theory of how organisations as different as firms, certifications, and state regulators have a common-but-differentiated influence on said challenges.

At the more practical level, I am the person you want when you need solutions for multi-dimensional policy/governance challenges involving many different actors. That is, of course, assuming you are willing to stick to the process of developing robust solutions. I do not believe in quick fixes.

Check this profile from my time at the LSE if you want to learn more about my approach to policy analysis.
