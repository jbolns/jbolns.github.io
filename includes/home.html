Dr Jose A Bolanos

Mildly extravagant political economist

Welcome! I am a data-savvy Political Economist with significant experience in analytical and data-driven roles across the academic, public, and private sectors.

I am a perspectivist. I believe that to truly understand reality and solve problems you need to look at things from many different angles. I also like to use different tools and methods, as required by the problem at hand.

In turn, I am who you want when there is a need to make sense and overcome very difficult challenges. That is, of course, assuming you are willing to stick to the process of developing robust solutions. I do not believe in quick fixes.
